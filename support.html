Support¶

Getting in Touch¶

Gitter

Feel free to join the RDTK gitter chat. Most of the time, at least one RDTK developer should be available there.

When using the chat, please be patient. We will likely not responds within seconds or minutes but within hours.

Reporting Bugs¶

Bug reports are always welcome on issue tracker.
