Support
=======

Getting in Touch
----------------

Gitter

  Feel free to join the |project| `gitter chat`_. Most of the time, at
  least one |project| developer should be available there.

  When using the chat, please be patient. We will likely not responds
  within seconds or minutes but within hours.

Reporting Bugs
--------------

Bug reports are always welcome on `issue tracker`_.
